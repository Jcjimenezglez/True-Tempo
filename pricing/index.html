Why I built Superfocus

Hey! I'm Julius. So, I made this app because I had a serious focus problem. Like, I'd sit down to work and literally five minutes later I'm on my phone scrolling through Instagram or whatever. Next thing I know, an hour's gone by and I haven't done anything.

So I was like, you know what? I'm just gonna build something super simple. You hit start, timer goes, some chill ambient sounds play in the background to keep you locked in. Need a break? Just pause it. That's literally it.

Turns out a bunch of college students started using it to study for exams and stuff. And freelancers like me use it to actually get client work done without losing focus every two seconds.

No crazy features, no BS. Just something that actually helps you stay focused and get stuff done.

Anyway, yeah. That's it.
