What is Superfocus?

Superfocus is a pomodoro timer app that helps you focus and get more done. Simple time management‚Äîjust press start and focus.

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method: focus for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break, repeat. After 4 sessions, take a 15-minute break. Learn more ‚Üí

Benefits

  • Stay focused - 25-minute sessions keep you in deep work mode
  • Avoid burnout - Regular breaks keep your energy high
  • Track progress - See exactly how much you accomplish

How to Use

  1. Pick your task
  2. Start the timer and focus for 25 minutes
  3. Take a 5-minute break
  4. Repeat 4 times, then take a longer break
